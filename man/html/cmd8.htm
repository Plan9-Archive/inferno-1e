date - print the date

date

Description

 Prints the date to standard output, in the format:

Fri Feb 16 17:03:52 CDT 1997

Current time is obtained from reading /dev/time. The conversion from Greenwich Mean Time to local time depends on the contents of /locale/timezone.

Files

/locale/timezone

/dev/time
